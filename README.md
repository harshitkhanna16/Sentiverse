# Sentiverse

Sentiverse is an interactive emotional wellness app built with SwiftUI that visualizes human emotions as a dynamic universe.
Instead of displaying emotions as simple text or statistics, Sentiverse transforms emotional states into a cosmic experience where the Earth represents the user’s inner emotional state, and surrounding celestial elements react to different moods.
The app encourages users to reflect on their feelings and perform small, meaningful actions to restore emotional balance.

# Core Concept

In Sentiverse, emotions are represented through changes in a cosmic environment:
1. Anger
The Sun moves closer to Earth, causing the planet to overheat.
Users perform guided breathing exercises to cool the system and restore balance.
2. Sadness
The Earth dims while the Moon begins to orbit around it.
Users are encouraged to reach out and connect with loved ones.
3. Stress
The Earth's light fades under pressure.
Users restore its brightness by logging positive memories and experiences.
4. Confusion
The Earth disappears into the cosmic void.
Users regain clarity by defining a goal or direction.
5. Happiness
The universe flourishes.
Users spread positivity by planting virtual trees, helping the Earth thrive.

# Features

1. Emotion-driven cosmic environment
2. Earth as a visual representation of emotional state
3. Guided breathing exercises
4. Mood-based animations and interactions
5. Positive action prompts
6. Immersive UI built with SwiftUI
7. Personal emotional reflection experience

# Purpose

Sentiverse aims to transform emotional regulation into an engaging visual journey.
By combining storytelling, animations, and interactive exercises, the app helps users:
1. Reflect on their emotions
2. Restore mental balance
3. Build healthier emotional habits
